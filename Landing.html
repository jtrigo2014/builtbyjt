What is this training system?

This program uses a 3-week rotating intensity cycle developed by elite bodybuilding coach an elite bodybuilding coach. It uses a 3-week rotating intensity cycle — Week 1 is heavy and low rep, Week 2 is moderate, Week 3 is high rep and metabolic. This rotation prevents adaptation and maximizes muscle growth over time.

Do I need to download anything?

No. The app runs entirely in your browser. You can save it to your phone's home screen and it works like a native app — no App Store, no downloads required.

Is this suitable for beginners?

Training is an intermediate to advanced program. If you've been training consistently for at least 6 months and understand compound lifts, this program will take you to the next level. Complete beginners should build a foundation first.

How does the macro calculator work?

It uses a leading sports nutrition researcher' formula from The Muscle & Strength Nutrition Pyramid: your bodyweight × 10 as a baseline, multiplied by an activity multiplier. From there it sets protein, fat, and carbs based on whether you're cutting, bulking, or maintaining — all based on peer-reviewed research.
