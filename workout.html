📌 Daily Foundation
Multivitamin
Cut
Essential
1 per day — normal dosed (not mega-dosed bodybuilding formulas)
Mainly during a cut when calories are restricted. Not necessary during a bulk if diet is varied. Look for USP or NSF certified.
Fish Oil (EPA + DHA)
Cut & Bulk
Essential
2–3g/day of EPA + DHA combined
Check the label — add EPA + DHA values together. Reduces inflammation, aids joint recovery, supports mood and hormones. Algae oil works if you don't eat fish.
Vitamin D3
Cut & Bulk
Essential
9–36 IU/lb/day (20–80 IU/kg/day) based on sun exposure
Deficiency is far more common than most think and negatively impacts muscle performance and hormones. Get bloodwork if possible.
⚡ Performance
Creatine Monohydrate
Cut & Bulk
Essential
0.018g/lb/day (~3–5g/day) — no loading needed
Most researched, most effective, most affordable supplement. Monohydrate only — all fancy variants are inferior. Look for CreaPure certified. Takes 2–4 weeks to fully saturate.
Caffeine
Cut & Bulk
Situational
Tiredness: 0.45–1.4mg/lb · Performance: 1.81–2.72mg/lb, max 2x/week
Only pre-workout Helms recommends. Use sparingly — tolerance kills the performance effect fast. Start at half the dose if new to stimulants.
🔬 Situational
Beta-Alanine
Cut & Bulk
Conditional
3–4g/day
Only beneficial for sets of 15+ reps or cardio intervals over 60 seconds. No benefit for strength work. Skip if most of your training is heavy.
BCAAs
Cut
Conditional
0.09g/lb (0.2g/kg) pre-workout — only if fasted, low-carb, or glycogen depleted
Not needed if protein intake is already high. Evidence is weak. Only consider if training fasted or in severe caloric restriction.
HMB
Cut
Conditional
3g/day pre-workout — cutting only
May help preserve muscle during aggressive cuts. Not needed when bulking. Only consider if you have budget left after the essentials above.
